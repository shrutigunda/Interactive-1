My father took
                             One hundred
                               And thirty-two minutes
                                 To die.
                                   I counted.
                                     It happened on the Jellicoe Road.
                                       The prettiest road I'd ever seen,
                                         Where trees made breezy canopies
                                           Like a tunnel to Shangri-La.
                                             We were going to the ocean,
                                               Hundreds of miles away,
                                                 Because I wanted to see the ocean
                                               And my father said that it was about time
                                             The four of us made that journey.
                                           I remember asking,
                                         "What's the difference
                                       Between a trip and a journey?"
                                     And my father said,
                                   "Narnie, my love,
                                 When we get there,
                               You'll understand,"
                             And that was the last thing
                           He ever said.

                           We heard her almost straightaway.
                         In the other car,
                       Wedged into ours so deep
                     That you couldn't tell where one began and the other ended.
                   She told us her name was Tate
             And then she squeezed through the glass and the steel
           And climbed over her own dead-
         -Just to be with Webb and me;
       To give us her hand
     So we could clutch it
   With all our might.
 And then a kid called Fitz came
   Riding by on a stolen bike
     And saved our lives.
       Someone asked us later,
         "Didn't you wonder
           Why no one came across you sooner?"
             Did I wonder?
               When you see your parents zipped up
                 In black body bags on the Jellicoe Road
                   Like they're some kind of garbage,
                     Don't you know?
                       Wonder dies.
